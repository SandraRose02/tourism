KAPPAD BEACH

Step onto the historic shores of Kappad Beach, where Vasco da Gama first set foot in India in 1498. Enjoy the serene ambiance, golden sands, and breathtaking sunsets, making it a perfect spot for leisurely strolls and relaxation by the Arabian Sea.

BEYPORE

Explore the ancient port town of Beypore, known for its traditional shipbuilding industry and historical significance. Witness the craftsmanship of traditional Uru boats, visit the centuries-old Beypore Fort, and stroll along the scenic Beypore Beach, offering a glimpse into Kerala's maritime heritage.

KOZHIKODE BEACH

Unwind at the popular Kozhikode Beach, also known as Calicut Beach, offering a perfect blend of scenic beauty and recreational activities. Enjoy leisurely walks along the sandy shores, indulge in delicious street food from local vendors, and witness mesmerizing sunsets over the Arabian Sea. Explore nearby attractions like the iconic lighthouse and the historical piers, adding to the charm of this coastal destination.
