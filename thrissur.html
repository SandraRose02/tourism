ATHIRAPPILY

Marvel at the cascading beauty of Athirappilly, the largest waterfall in Kerala, nestled amidst lush greenery. Enjoy the breathtaking views, take a refreshing dip in the natural pools, and explore the surrounding forest trails, teeming with diverse flora and fauna.

GURUVAYUR TEMPLE

Pay homage at the revered Guruvayur Sri Krishna Temple, one of the most important pilgrimage centers for Hindus in South India. Witness the rituals, admire the intricate architecture, and soak in the spiritual ambiance of this sacred site dedicated to Lord Krishna.

PEECHI DAM

Escape to the serene environs of Peechi, where the majestic Peechi Dam reservoir awaits. Indulge in boating activities, surrounded by scenic hills and dense forests. Explore the adjoining wildlife sanctuary, home to diverse species of birds, butterflies, and mammals, making it a paradise for nature lovers and birdwatchers alike.
