KUMARAKOM

Immerse yourself in the serene beauty of the Kumarakom Backwaters, a labyrinth of canals, lagoons, and waterways adorned with lush greenery. Enjoy a leisurely houseboat cruise, savoring the tranquil surroundings and glimpses of village life along the banks

VEMBANAD LAKE

Explore the largest lake in Kerala, Vembanad Lake, renowned for its scenic charm and vibrant ecosystem. Engage in activities like boating, fishing, and birdwatching, spotting migratory birds and indigenous species amidst the picturesque landscape

ARUVIKKUZHI WATERFALLS

Escape to the tranquil beauty of Aruvikkuzhi Waterfalls, nestled amidst dense rubber plantations and picturesque landscapes. Enjoy the soothing sound of cascading water, picnic by the waterfall, and unwind in the serene natural surroundings.
