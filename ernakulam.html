FORT KOCHI

Fort Kochi is steeped in colonial history, with iconic Chinese fishing nets, historic St. Francis Church, and the Dutch Palace showcasing Kerala's rich heritage. It's also a cultural hub, hosting events like the Kochi Biennale.

CHERAI BEACH

Cherai Beach, situated on Vypin Island, entices visitors with its golden sands, serene ambiance, and coconut palm-lined shores. It's ideal for leisurely strolls, sunbathing, and water sports like surfing and jet skiing.

MARINE DRIVE

Marine Drive is a picturesque waterfront promenade along Kochi's backwaters, offering panoramic views, well-maintained walkways, and recreational areas. It's perfect for relaxing evenings, boat rides, and soaking in Kochi's coastal charm.
